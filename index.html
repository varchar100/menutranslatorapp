Menu Translator Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7/8/2025

Overview

Menu Translator is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

Menu Translator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. We do not require account creation, email addresses, or any other personal identifiers to use the app.

Image Processing

When you scan a menu using the camera feature, the app uses Google's Gemini API to detect and translate text from the captured image.

Important: No images are stored on your device or on Menu Translator's servers. Images are processed in real-time for text recognition and translation purposes only, then immediately discarded.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google's Gemini API for optical character recognition (OCR) and translation services. When you scan a menu, the image data is sent to Google's servers for processing according to Google's privacy policy.

We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Local Storage

The app may store your language preferences and settings locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not shared with Menu Translator or any third parties.

Camera Permissions

The app requires camera access to scan restaurant menus. Camera access is used solely for capturing images for text recognition and translation. No photos are saved to your device's photo library without your explicit consent.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored by Menu Translator, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@menutranslator.app
